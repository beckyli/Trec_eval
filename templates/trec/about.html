Welcome to the TREC Evaluator



TREC stands for Text Retrieval Conference and is focuses on different Information Retrieval methodologies. Essentially, it allows different ideas on fast Information Retrieval techniques to be collected and used to aid future research.

Through TREC EVAL, you can view the current standings on who has contributed towards these different research areas, known as tracks. The aim is to raise awareness of what TREC is and so this app allows everyone to see how different areas of research in Information Retrieval are advancing.
